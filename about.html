About DonationTrack

DonationTrack is a transparent giving platform designed to help connect people in need with generous donors. Whether it's for medical expenses, education, or emergencies, our mission is to make every donation meaningful and trackable.

We provide a virtual card for every fundraiser so donors can track exactly how their money is being used — offering a level of trust and visibility that traditional platforms don’t always guarantee.

This is a prototype web project focused on learning, development, and exploring how modern technologies can make giving more transparent, accessible, and impactful.
